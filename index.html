Colors

RGB

Format: rgb(red, green, blue)

Example: rgb(255, 0, 0) - This produces a bright red color. The values range for 0 to 255, with 255 indicating full intensity of color.

Description: Think of RGB as mixing light. When all colors are at full intensity (255), you get white. When all are at 0, you get black.
